§ THE FINE PRINT

Everything you'd want.
Nothing you don't.

01

Works in every app

Reads your selection through the macOS Accessibility API, with a synthetic-copy fallback for Electron & web apps — and your clipboard is always restored afterward.

02

Private by default

Run fully on-device with Ollama. Nothing leaves your Mac unless you deliberately choose a cloud model. Works on a plane; no per-token bill.

03

Undo-safe rewrite

Rewrites are pasted in place and preserve each app's native undo stack. Changed your mind? ⌘Z, any time.

04

Auto or fixed direction

Detects the selected language and translates both ways — set independently for ⌥D and ⌥R. Or pin a fixed direction when mixed-language text could trip detection.

05

Four tones

Faithful, Formal, Casual, or Polished — chosen independently for reading and rewriting, across the ten most-spoken languages.

06

Menu-bar native

No Dock clutter. Global shortcuts, launch at login, and a per-app skip list for the apps where you'd rather it stayed quiet.
